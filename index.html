My cat's poop is glittery?

July 7 2018

Lately, I've noticed something peculiar about my cat’s litter box. Among the usual evidence of feline activity, there are shimmering, glittery bits in her poop. It’s as if she's consumed stardust or taken a whimsical turn through a magical realm. I've racked my brain and scoured the house for possible culprits, yet the mystery deepens. Is she secretly a fairy? A cosmic traveler in disguise? Each scoop only adds to the enigma. What could be the source of this unexpected sparkle in my cat's daily business? The answer remains elusive, leaving me both puzzled and enchanted.

Whiskers brought me a key.

May 15 2004

There’s something inexplicably eerie about Whiskers lately. His behavior has taken on an air of mystery that I can’t quite put my finger on. At night, he sits by the window, staring intently at the moon as if communicating with it. Shadows seem to follow him, even in well-lit rooms, and sometimes I catch a glimmer of something otherworldly in his eyes. Last week, a small, ancient-looking key appeared in his favorite sleeping spot. Where did it come from? What secrets does my enigmatic feline hold? Whiskers, it seems, is far more than an ordinary cat. Do you have any suggestions, where I should try out the key? I've tried it at the front door, but so far, no luck.

Luna's Treasures

April 15 2001

Luna, my sleek black cat, has always been a creature of habit—until recently. It started with the small, inexplicable gifts she began leaving at my door: a tarnished locket, a crumpled map, and a tiny, weathered book with pages too faded to read. I have no idea where she's finding these relics. Each item seems to whisper tales of long-lost adventures. Luna watches me closely as I examine each piece, her emerald eyes gleaming with secrets. Is she guiding me toward something, or is she merely a collector of forgotten histories? The mystery of Luna’s treasures deepens with each passing day.
